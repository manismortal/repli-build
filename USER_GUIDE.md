# User & Admin Guide

## Getting Started
Navigate to the homepage and click "Register" to create an account.
*   **Note:** The **first user** to register on the platform is automatically granted **Administrator** privileges.

## For Users

### Dashboard
The dashboard shows your current balance, total earnings, and quick actions.

### Making a Deposit
1.  Go to **Wallet** -> **Deposit** (or click the Bkash icon).
2.  Enter the amount you wish to deposit.
3.  Enter your Bkash number and PIN (Simulation).
4.  Click **Confirm**.
5.  Your request will be submitted as **Pending**.
6.  Once an Admin approves it, your **Main Balance** will be updated.

### Withdrawals
1.  Go to **Wallet**.
2.  Select "Withdraw".
3.  Enter the amount.
4.  The request will be sent to Admins for approval.

### Tasks & Products
- Browse available packages in **Products**.
- Purchase packages to increase your daily earning potential.
- Complete **Tasks** to earn rewards.

## For Admins

### Accessing the Admin Panel
Log in with your admin account. You will see an "Admin" button in the sidebar or navigation menu. Alternatively, go to `/admin`.

### Dashboard Overview
View real-time statistics:
- Total Users
- Pending Withdrawals
- Active Packages
- Total Deposits & Payouts

### Managing Deposits
1.  Navigate to **Deposits** in the Admin Sidebar.
2.  You will see a list of all deposit requests with Transaction IDs.
3.  Click **Check (âœ“)** to Approve. The user's wallet balance will increase immediately.
4.  Click **X** to Reject invalid requests.

### Managing Withdrawals
1.  Navigate to **Withdrawals**.
2.  Review the user and amount.
3.  Approve or Reject the request.
    *   *Note:* Rejecting a withdrawal does not currently refund the balance automatically in this version (unless implemented), so use with care or manually adjust balance if needed.

### User Management
- Go to **Users** to view all registered users and their current balances.

### Package Management
- Go to **Packages** to Create, Edit, or Delete product packages available for purchase.
